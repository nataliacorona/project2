Chicken Salad Tostadas

Ingredients

  • 1 chicken breast, skinless & boneless
  • 2 tablespoons of mayo
  • 1 tablespoon sour cream
  • 1/4 cup diced carrots
  • 1/4 cup diced celery
  • 1/4 cup diced potatoes
  • 1/4 cup diced onion
  • tostadas
  • Tapatio sauce -optional

Preparation and Cooking Time

  • Cooking time: 20 minutes
  • Preparation time: 5 minutes

Step 1

In a large pot with filtered water, add chicken breast, carrots, celery, onion, and potatoes. Bring to a boil and cover with lid. Let it cook for approx. 20 minutes.

Step 2

Remove chicken and vegetables from water and drain. Allow all ingredients to cool down. Once chicken is cooled, shred chicken with 2 forks.

Step 3

Add all cooked ingredients into a large bowl. Add sour cream and mayo to the bowl and lightly mix all ingredients together.

Step 4

Add a scoop of the salad to a tostada, add Tapatio sauce on top, and enjoy!
